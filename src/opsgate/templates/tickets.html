Role model

OpsGate currently supports exactly three workflow roles. Use them to keep tickets inspect-only, repo-first, and review-driven.

investigator

Inspect state, collect evidence, identify the owning repo/workspace, and propose next steps. No edits, deploys, or ad hoc live fixes.

implementer

Make the change in the source of truth, iterate with review, validate it, then commit, deploy, and verify when the ticket calls for a live fix.

reviewer

Review the proposed work, validation, logs, and rollout plan. Do not make independent implementation or deployment changes here.
